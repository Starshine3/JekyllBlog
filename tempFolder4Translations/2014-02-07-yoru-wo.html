English:

The bad morning which I awoke to, fell from the sky
The boring night disappeared to somewhere.
Even though I am always thinking about these kind of things,
At the end of yesterday's dream, I saw a small light.

It wasn't lonely, and it wasn't fun.
But I saw everything change.
I am trying to find
The continuation of the dream I saw yesterday,
And the lamp that lit up the road.
That is all.
Probably, I am expecting
A world that is in different colors than today's world,
With light that smiles all the time.
I hope all will happen soon!
On my journey to find the light, with lots of interest I advance.
It became all that I want, and so I threw away everything else.

There was loneliness, there was intoxicating fun.
But I saw everything change.

I am trying to find
The art of going against the withering days,
And the medicine that can heal the spreading of pain.
That is all.
However, I
Became too familiar with the withering days
And the pains before unknown are increasing.
I am truly an idiot, I want to cry..
If I become aware that I am all alone,
Even my foot would become completely useless.
I would then want to end it all.
But- I am expecting
A world that is in different colors than today's world,
With light that smiles all the time.
I hope all will happen soon!
