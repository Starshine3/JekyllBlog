背景、夏に溺れる

(Haikei, Natsu ni Oboreru)

Scenery, Indulging in Summer


Composition, Lyrics, Music: nabuna
Vocal: Hatsune Miku Append (Dark)
English

Perhaps because you lost favor and faith in me, our relationship decayed
I lit a flame to an umbrella (the 19th). Again today

A little past 12 o’clock it fell on the asphalt,
The burden upon your tiny life. The flame is gentle

When the rain paused, the morning resulted in indigo
A feather of fluorescent color hangs onto your wet hand

Summer cicadas fill the ends of the sky
Their fading song dissolves in my ear

Your voice resounds from a corner of summer
And swims through the town like a fish

I cling onto the credit roll. Once again today,
I’m by myself. Doing anything to cling onto your breath. Tomorrow too

Even if others’ voices pile up we stay oblivious
Even forgetting that we stood up from our chairs

The bouquet of summer is in the shadow of the wind chime
Drowning in water, your words are distorted

In a corner of the evening
Your shadow is still wavering in the sky

Underneath the umbrella there’s a cicada shell
It will eventually disappear, along with the cries of the cicada

When the crows cries, let’s go back home
And disturb the sun-fallen town. So that we won’t cry for the sunset

The glow of a firefly, the sunset in the sky
Summer ended. But your voice only

Blurred a little, faded a little
Into the back of a madder flower

Dusk fills the limits of the sky
My fading dreams repaints over you

Now, the cicada’s song stopped and blurred.
The crows cry underneath the torii

You smile. At some corner of summer
