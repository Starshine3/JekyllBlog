東京レトロ
(Toukyou retoro)

Toyko Retro

Composition, Arrangement, Lyrics：sukoppu すこっぷ 
Vocal: Hatsune Miku

English

Taking my future and my heart on a ride
The train travels, heading toward anywhere
The city’s wind is tender as love
Making our hearts throb everyday

I wonder what is in that place?
Ginza, Yotsuya, and even Kourakuen
Any unknown roads with blooming flower
Is a favorable route!

(Aiya)

Where shall I go today?
The pleasant wind blows
Oh yeah! I’ll quickly dress up
And go and meet my lover

Because this is such a small thing, it gets around
And connect people together
It is such a trivial thing, yet it is also so important
So let’s join hands and walk

It is a part of life to fall or fail
At work, in love, in self-improvement
At those times when everything is going badly
Let your tears stream

(Aiya)

What is in store for me today?
I’m having a bad time right now
But somehow, the sceneries that I see
Are slowly transforming into multiple colors

(Poppo-)

Forget about that day’s mistakes
Buy a ticket, climb up the stairs, and arrive at the train platform
Try to get going. See-
Something is changing in the air

(Aiya)

What is in store for me today?
The pleasant wind blows
I wish I will stay connected to these days
Even in the future

Where shall I go today?
Taking love’s aura and my imagination on a ride
My feelings are meant to be conveyed
So right now, I will go and meet you
