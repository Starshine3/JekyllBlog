人生は吠える
(Jinsei wa hoeru)

-Life is Howling-

Music: Neru
Vocal: Rib
Album: Riboot

English:

"Things always somehow gets better."
For years I was neglected. Everyone feigned ignorance, as if they can’t see.
And so I lived idly and grew up this way, this is life to me.

There should be some other methods,
But unfortunately I don’t have any guts and discipline; I can’t be helped.
In this way my dislike grew, so I abandoned my master.

If only these kinds of days would continue, when I can plant seeds of annoyance in those who ignored me.
This is all your fault you dumbheads. I should just throw you and people like you into ditches like that one.

Nobody noticed me. With a life running out of gas and this singing voice,
I don’t want to be laughed for my mistakes. So I will always howl.

Recently life has been quite awful,
I have a limit to ridding my troubles. I’m not affluent, it’s not like that,
I don’t want to think that nothing can be done, but my hands are all full,

I should just find better foster parents, and peek at them when they’re prepping the cardboard,
Wait this is a bit cowardlike, if I can only get through this without crying,
But even with my wet face I have no home here,

Nobody adopted me. With the lowest rank of life and this singing voice,
I fight. I don’t want to become nothing. So I will always howl.

Always scared of the words right in front of your eyes, you always evaluated and then underestimated others’ lives
I have come so far since crawling on the rolling floor. That’s right, we’re always so
Uneasy and nihilistic, with the worst life and this singing voice.
Until even this collar is torn to pieces, We will always howl.
