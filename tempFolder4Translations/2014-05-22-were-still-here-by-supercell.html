We’re Still Here

Lyrics & Composition: ryo (supercell)
Album: ZIGAEXPERIENTIA
Vocal: Koeda

English:

You cannot give away this ticket to others.
Even if you don't want to, you will embark on a journey by yourself.
You will intersect with various people, and then you will become aware
that you already knew the being that is yourself.

You are distrustful of kindness.
At times you were betrayed. At times you were hurt. But if you hate yourself because of this-

There is no one reason to explain why you are who you are.
You don't need to separate everything in black and white.
It's okay if you are lost, because you will eventually remember.

After the rain there was a rainbow,
even though that you struggled to reach that place,
by the time you arrived, it was gone.

You hated them all: dreams and wishes.
"They only cause sadness, they never get granted."
If you say that, then-

Please consider the people who you love and who love you back.
Because of you, they are able to be happy.
These people always exist somewhere in this world without exceptions.

Ah, walking by yourself,
you will soon be able to meet these kind of people one by one.
Awkwardly, you will take their hands,
but someday, you will be connected to them.
Even if you know that this connection is going to eventually crumble down, be unafraid.
There won't be anything to erase; this is the path that only you had taken.
Because we're still here.
So that's why, let's go and see tomorrow,
in this world in which you exist.
